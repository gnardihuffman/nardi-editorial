About Wendy Nardi


Wendy Nardi graduated from the University of Virginia with a bachelor's degree in English and French and from Columbia University with a Master's degree in Philosophy. With experience as a writer and an English adjunct at the University of Connecticut and Emerson College, Boston, and through her work at Nardi Editorial, Wendy has developed a time-tested set of literary capabilities that can be leveraged to suit client goals. Based out of her home in Connecticut, Wendy has worked with clients across the US, the UK, Canada, India, China, Thailand, the UAE and other countries. Her writing experience includes treatments and scripts for film/tv and podcast documentaries, feature writing for newspapers and magazines, and other projects.
